DEGANWY , UK

Deganwy is swish and sophisticated. On the shores of the Conwy Estuary between Victorian Llandudno and medieval Conwy, it enjoys a privileged position with stunning views across the water to Conwy Castle and the Isle of Anglesey. There’s a sand and stone beach and – Deganwy’s prized feature – a sleek, modern marina and is also noted for its stylish shopping and dining with a good choice of boutiques and restaurants
